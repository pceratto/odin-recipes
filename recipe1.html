Alton Brown's Aged Eggnog

Ingredients

  • 12 large eggs
  • 1 pound sugar
  • 1 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 pint half-and-half
  • 1 pint whole milk
  • 1 pint heavy cream
  • 1 cup rum
  • 1 cup cognac
  • 1 cup bourbon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Separate the eggs and store the whites.
  2. Beat the yolks with sugar and nutmeg.
  3. Combine dairy, booze, and salt then slowly beat into egg mixture
  4. Move into jar and store in the fridge for a minimum of 2 weeks.
  5. Serve chilled with extra nutmeg grated on top.
