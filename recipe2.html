Chef John's Shakshuka

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 1 teaspon salt
  • 1 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1 jalapeno pepper
  • 1 28 ounce can crushed tomato
  • 6 large eggs

Directions

  1. Sautee onions with oil and salt.
  2. Add peppers and continue to sautee.
  3. Stir in tomatoes and water as needed.
  4. Make an indentation for the eggs and place eggs in pot.
  5. Cover and cook until eggs reach desired doneness.
